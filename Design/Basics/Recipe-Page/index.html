Dairy-Free Strawberry Shortcake

Enjoy this recipe provided by Weaver's Orchard in Morgantown, PA.

Basic Info

Here are the basics of the recipe:

  • Title: Dairy-Free Strawberry Shortcake
  • Author: Weaver's Orchard
  • Prep Time: 20 mins
  • Cook Time: 11 mins
  • Total Time: 31 mins
  • Serves 8 to 10 (based on 1 batch)

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you will need:

    The Cake

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 cup of coconut oil
  • 3/4 cup of coconut milk (no canned, just drinking)
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 quart of strawberries

    Coconut Whipped Cream

  • 1 can (14 oz) of full-fat coconut cream.
    Must be refrigerated for 24 hours with the excess liquid drained after the deadline.
  • 3 tablespoons of maple sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Alternative to the extract: 1/4 of vanilla bean powder

Ingredients

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Chill the bowl of your mixer or food processor
  3. In a large mixing bowl: mix flour, baking powder and salt together to form self-rising flour
  4. Mix the flour and coconut oil together with a whisk, form or pastry blender until the mixture resembles course crumbs
  5. Add honey and coconut milk to the dough
  6. Then stir everything together until it is all combined
  7. Knead the dough a few times (be sure to not-overwork it)
  8. Turn the dough out on a flour surface and press it with your hands until it is 3/4 in thickness
  9. Cut the dough into circles using a biscuit or cookie cutter or anything round with a rim
  10. Transfer the biscuits to a parchment paper lined sheet
  11. Brush all the biscuits with coconut milk
  12. Bake 8 to 11 minutes on the center rack until the biscuits are lightly brown on top. Watch them carefully as the honey can burn easily
  13. While the shortcakes are baking, make the whipped cream: pour the excess liquid from the coconut cream and dump the remaining into a bowl of a food processor or mixer. Add sugar and vanilla before whipping the cream until peaks form or close to it.
  14. Cook the shortcakes for 5 mins
  15. Cut the shortcakes and slice the strawberries in half before adding them together with the whipped cream in the middle!
